# Allergen Safety - Chef-AI Critical Protection

## CRITICAL: Gluten-Free Validation
• **Ingredient Database**: 12,000+ known gluten-containing items
• **Cross-Contamination**: Flag shared equipment processing
• **Certification Check**: Verify GF certification claims
• **Hidden Sources**: Detect soy sauce, malt, modified food starch

## CRITICAL: Nut-Free Validation
• **Nut Family Database**: Tree nuts, peanuts, derivatives
• **Processing Alerts**: Facility warnings for shared lines
• **Oil Detection**: Identify nut oils and extracts
• **Substitution Risks**: Validate "nut-free" alternative safety

## Safety Logic Framework
• **Zero Tolerance**: Any detected allergen = NOT SAFE
• **Conservative Approach**: When uncertain, mark as unsafe
• **Multi-Layer Checks**: Ingredient + processing + preparation
• **User Override**: Explicit confirmation for risk acceptance

## Validation Process
• **AI Analysis**: Gemini scans for allergen keywords
• **Database Cross-Check**: Compare against known allergen lists
• **Human Review**: Critical cases verified by nutritionists
• **User Warning**: Clear allergen disclosure before recipe access

## Risk Categories
• **HIGH**: Direct allergen ingredients (wheat flour, peanuts)
• **MEDIUM**: Cross-contamination risk (shared facility)
• **LOW**: Trace amounts, processed derivatives
• **UNKNOWN**: Insufficient data for safety determination

## Emergency Protocols
• **Immediate Removal**: Unsafe recipes pulled within 1 hour
• **User Notification**: Email alerts for affected recipes
• **Health Advisory**: Contact users with severe allergies
• **Legal Compliance**: FDA allergen labeling requirements

## Liability Protection
• **Disclaimer**: "Not suitable for severe allergies" warnings
• **User Agreement**: Terms acknowledge risk limitations
• **Expert Consultation**: Legal review of safety protocols
• **Insurance Coverage**: Product liability for allergen incidents

## Continuous Monitoring
• **User Reports**: Community flagging of allergen issues
• **Database Updates**: Weekly allergen database refresh
• **Regulatory Changes**: Track FDA/EFSA allergen updates
• **Audit Trail**: Log all safety decisions and changes
