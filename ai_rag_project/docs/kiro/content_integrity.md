# Content Integrity - Chef-AI Recipe Safety

## AI Recipe Safety Verification
• **Toxicity Detection**: Identify harmful ingredient combinations
• **Temperature Safety**: Validate cooking temperatures for food safety
• **Allergen Cross-Check**: Ensure allergen labeling accuracy
• **Nutrition Balance**: Detect dangerous nutritional deficiencies

## Safety Validation Layers
• **AI Analysis**: Gemini scans for safety red flags
• **Database Cross-Reference**: Compare against safe recipe patterns
• **Expert Review**: Professional chef validation for new recipes
• **User Feedback**: Community reporting of safety issues

## Critical Safety Checks
• **Raw Meat**: Ensure proper cooking temperatures
• **Cross-Contamination**: Flag unsafe food handling
• **Expiration Dates**: Warn about potentially unsafe ingredients
• **Dietary Restrictions**: Validate medical safety claims

## Unsafe Content Detection
• **Harmful Combinations**: Dangerous ingredient pairings
• **Incorrect Temperatures**: Undercooked food risks
• **Toxic Ingredients**: Identify poisonous plants/substances
• **Allergic Reactions**: Life-threatening allergen presence

## Verification Process
• **Automated Scanning**: Real-time safety analysis during recipe creation
• **Manual Review**: Human verification for high-risk recipes
• **Community Moderation**: User flagging system for safety issues
• **Expert Consultation**: Nutritionist review for medical claims

## Emergency Response
• **Immediate Removal**: Unsafe recipes removed within 30 minutes
• **User Notification**: Alert system for affected users
• **Health Warnings**: Clear safety disclaimers when needed
• **Regulatory Reporting**: Compliance with food safety authorities

## Quality Assurance
• **Recipe Testing**: Physical testing of AI-generated recipes
• **Accuracy Metrics**: Track safety prediction accuracy
• **False Positive Rate**: Minimize incorrect safety warnings
• **Continuous Improvement**: Machine learning from safety outcomes

## Legal Compliance
• **Food Safety Standards**: FDA and international regulations
• **Liability Protection**: Clear disclaimers and user agreements
• **Expert Documentation**: Professional validation records
• **Insurance Coverage**: Product liability for recipe safety
