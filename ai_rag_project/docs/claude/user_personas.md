# User Personas - Chef-AI Core Users

## Busy Parents
• **Demographics**: Ages 30-45, 2+ children, dual-income households
• **Pain Points**: Limited time, picky eaters, budget constraints
• **Goals**: Quick healthy meals, family-friendly recipes, easy shopping
• **Behavior**: Search by time (<30 min), dietary restrictions, batch cooking
• **Needs**: Meal planning, leftover ideas, kid-approved recipes
• **Usage Patterns**: Evening browsing, weekend meal prep, mobile-first

## Professional Chefs
• **Demographics**: Ages 25-50, culinary trained, restaurant industry
• **Pain Points**: Recipe inspiration, cost management, staff training
• **Goals**: Creative techniques, ingredient sourcing, menu development
• **Behavior**: Advanced search, technique tutorials, plating ideas
• **Needs**: Precision scaling, cost calculations, seasonal ingredients
• **Usage Patterns**: Desktop research, recipe development, staff sharing

## Health-Conscious Millennials
• **Demographics**: Ages 25-35, fitness-focused, single/couples
• **Pain Points**: Meal prep boredom, nutrition tracking, social eating
• **Goals**: Macro tracking, variety, Instagram-worthy meals
• **Behavior**: Filter by diet, calorie counting, trendy ingredients
• **Needs**: Nutrition info, portion control, meal prep schedules
• **Usage Patterns**: Mobile app usage, social sharing, weekend planning

## Beginner Cooks
• **Demographics**: Ages 18-30, limited cooking experience
• **Pain Points**: Technique confusion, ingredient fear, failure anxiety
• **Goals**: Basic skills, confidence building, simple recipes
• **Behavior**: Step-by-step guides, video tutorials, beginner filters
• **Needs**: Technique explanations, substitution help, troubleshooting
• **Usage Patterns**: Guided cooking, skill progression, recipe ratings

## Business Priorities
• **Primary Focus**: Busy Parents (largest user segment, highest engagement)
• **Growth Opportunity**: Health-Conscious Millennials (social sharing potential)
• **Premium Market**: Professional Chefs (willing to pay for advanced features)
• **Long-term**: Beginner Cooks (lifetime value through skill development)

## Product Strategy
• **Core Features**: Time-saving tools for parents
• **Social Features**: Sharing capabilities for millennials
• **Professional Tools**: Advanced features for chefs
• **Educational Content**: Skill-building for beginners
