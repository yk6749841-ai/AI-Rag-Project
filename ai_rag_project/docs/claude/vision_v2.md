# Vision V2 - Chef-AI Product Roadmap

## Smart Shopping Lists
• **Auto-Generation**: Create lists from selected recipes automatically
• **Pantry Integration**: Check existing ingredients before shopping
• **Store Mapping**: Organize items by grocery store layout
• **Budget Tracking**: Calculate total cost and suggest alternatives
• **Multi-Store Support**: Compare prices across different retailers

## AI Meal Planning
• **Weekly Planning**: Generate 7-day meal schedules automatically
• **Dietary Preferences**: Customize for allergies, restrictions, goals
• **Seasonal Ingredients**: Prioritize fresh, in-season produce
• **Leftover Optimization**: Use leftovers in future meal plans
• **Family Scheduling**: Coordinate meals around family activities

## Smart Features Rollout
• **Q1 2024**: Basic shopping list generation
• **Q2 2024**: Store price comparisons and budget tracking
• **Q3 2024**: AI meal planning with dietary customization
• **Q4 2024**: Family coordination and seasonal optimization

## User Value Proposition
• **Time Savings**: Reduce meal planning from hours to minutes
• **Cost Reduction**: Optimize shopping and minimize food waste
• **Stress Relief**: Eliminate daily "what's for dinner" decisions
• **Health Goals**: Support dietary objectives automatically

## Business Impact
• **User Engagement**: Increase daily active users by 40%
• **Retention**: Improve 30-day retention through habit formation
• **Revenue**: Premium tier for advanced meal planning features
• **Partnerships**: Grocery store affiliate programs
